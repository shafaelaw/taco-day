The Details

What's going down 🌿

Taco Day is a little tradition to celebrate the summer solstice together, making the most of the longest day of the year. The name is a nod to past celebrations that famously involved tacos, but what it's always been about is good company, outdoors. We'd love to see you!

What to bring 🧺

We'll provide some food and beverages and will share details once we have RSVPs. You're welcome to bring any snacks or drinks you love, especially if you have specific dietary needs or favorites. Coming in from out of town? No pressure to bring a thing. If you do plan to bring something, mention it in your RSVP so we can coordinate.

How to prepare 🧥

We'll be on grass, so bring your favorite picnic blanket or lawn chairs. Public restrooms are available nearby. A summer solstice in Golden Gate Park has a personality of its own (some say the coldest winter is a summer in San Francisco), so check the forecast and dress in layers. You'll likely need a jacket and sunscreen. We like using Mr. Chilly on the day of, since weather in the Outer Richmond can be unpredictable. Pack for your comfort and you'll be golden. Not sure what to expect? Reach out with questions!
